# Technical Working Group

The Technical Working Group is a subgroup of the Community and consists of a
number of members who meet on a regular basis. The
[Technical Working Group members](#technical-working-group-members) are
responsible for topics such as:

* the project roadmap.
* the design and future development of the Open Education API specification
  (development follows an annual cycle consisting of a minor and a major
  release).
* project management.
* the implementation of new data.
* evaluation of work completed (new information implemented by SURF).
* promotion of OEAPI.
* the Community.

The Technical Working Group “way of working” is defined in the
[Governance](governance/) model, which includes detailed information such as:

* who may join the Technical Working Group.
* a list of affiliated institutions.
* how to become a member of the Technical Working Group.
* how to terminate membership of the Technical Working Group.
* the rights and obligations of a Technical Working Group member.

For more information on the Governance model, please consult the
[Governance](governance/) page.

To register for the Technical Working Group and become an active member of
the Community, please email us at info@openonderwijsapi.nl
